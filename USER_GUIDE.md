# User Guide: Managing Products

This guide explains how to add, edit, and publish products to the MM Earthmovers website.

## 0. First Time Setup (Prerequisites)
Before you can access the admin portal, you need to be authorized.

1.  **Create a GitHub Account**:
    *   Go to [github.com/join](https://github.com/join) and create a free account.
    *   Verify your email address.
2.  **Accept Invitation**:
    *   Send your GitHub username or email to the website owner.
    *   They will invite you as a collaborator.
    *   Check your email for an invitation from GitHub and click **Accept Invitation**.

## 1. Accessing the Admin Portal
1.  Go to your website URL and add `/admin` to the end (e.g., `https://mm-earthmovers.vercel.app/admin`).
2.  Click **Login with GitHub**.
3.  If asked, authorize the application.

## 2. Adding a New Product
1.  Click the **New Products** button (top right).
2.  Fill in the details:
    *   **Part Name**: The name of the product (e.g., "Turbo Charger").
    *   **Category**: Choose Loader, Motor Grader, or Excavator.
    *   **Brand/Model**: Select the specific brand (e.g., JCB, CAT). *Note: The options change based on the Category you selected above.*
    *   **Sizes Available**: Type sizes separated by commas (e.g., `10mm, 20mm`).
    *   **Product Image**: Click to upload an image from your computer.
    *   **Description**: Add any details about the product.
3.  **Save**: Click **Save** at the top.
    *   This saves the product as a **Draft**. It is NOT visible on the website yet.

## 3. Publishing (Making it Live)
To make your product visible to customers:
1.  Click the **Workflow** tab at the top of the page.
2.  You will see your product in the **Drafts** column.
3.  Drag the card to the **Ready** column.
4.  Click the **Publish** button on the card.
5.  Select **Publish now**.

The website will update automatically in a few minutes!

## 4. Editing or Deleting
*   **To Edit**: Click on any product in the list, make changes, and save. You will need to publish the changes again.
*   **To Delete**: Open a product and click the "Delete entry" button (usually at the bottom or in a settings menu).
